# NicheStream Community Guidelines for BlerdArt

**Effective Date:** May 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 2026  
**Community:** BlerdArt Creators & Viewers

---

## Our Mission

NicheStream is dedicated to building a safe, inclusive platform where Black creators and enthusiasts can celebrate, share, and monetize BlerdArt—Black art across all mediums: digital, traditional, animation, comics, and more.

We believe art is freedom. These guidelines protect that freedom while ensuring everyone can create and engage without fear of theft, harassment, or exploitation.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Respect Creation** — All work belongs to its creator
2. **Honor Community** — Black BlerdArt is our shared heritage
3. **Embrace Diversity** — All styles, tools, and mediums welcome
4. **Center Safety** — Protect vulnerable creators and voices
5. **Empower Accountability** — Creator verification and transparency

---

## What We Welcome

### Content
- ✓ Digital art (Procreate, Clip Studio, Photoshop, etc.)
- ✓ Traditional media (pen, paint, sculpture photography, etc.)
- ✓ Animation, comics, illustration, character design
- ✓ Afro-fantasy, sci-fi, and speculative Black futures
- ✓ Process videos, tutorials, speedpaints
- ✓ Constructive feedback and critique
- ✓ Community art circles and collaboration

- ✓ Supporting fellow creators with shares and kind comments
- ✓ Learning from others' work and techniques
- ✓ Offering honest critique with respect
- ✓ Celebrating diverse artistic expression
- ✓ Standing up against harassment or IP theft
- ✓ Taking time to recharge when community engagement feels overwhelming

## What We Don't Allow

### IP Theft & Plagiarism
- ✗ Posting someone else's work as your own
- ✗ Using artistic assets (brushes, models, code) without permission or attribution
- ✗ Claiming credit for ideas, techniques, or inspiration without acknowledgment
- ✗ Commercial use of copyrighted work without license
- ✗ "Reference" uploads that are primarily tracing or heavy copying

**Our Response:** Content removal, creator flagging, potential ban. All creators can report IP theft via the report menu on video pages.

### AI Art Without Disclosure
- ✗ Posting AI-generated art as human-created (see "Human-Created Affirm" checkbox on upload)
- ✗ Using AI assistants to generate primary content without labeling
- ✗ Mixing generative AI without transparency (e.g., "I generated backgrounds with [tool], then painted over them")

**Clarification:** We welcome AI as a *tool* (like Photoshop or Procreate), but not as a *creator substitute*. Disclose your AI usage.

**Our Response:** Unchecking the "Human-Created" checkbox removes that badge. Videos can remain public; transparency is the goal, not punishment.

### Harassment & Hate
- ✗ Slurs, dehumanizing language, or hateful stereotypes based on race, gender, sexuality, disability, or identity
- ✗ Threats, doxxing, or coordinated harassment
- ✗ Unsolicited explicit sexual content or sexual harassment
- ✗ Mocking, memes, or "criticism" targeting marginalized people
- ✗ Gatekeeping based on identity (e.g., "real Black artists don't use Photoshop")

**Our Response:** Warning → video restriction → creator suspension → permanent ban (depending on severity). Targets of harassment connect to resources + support from moderators.

### Spam & Exploitation
- ✗ Repeatedly posting low-effort content to game algorithm
- ✗ Fake engagement (bots, purchased followers, artificial comments)
- ✗ External links to scams, malware, or MLM schemes
- ✗ Soliciting payment outside the platform (except creator-to-fan relationships already established)
- ✗ Posting others' streams/videos without permission

## Creator Verification & Safety

### BlerdArt Creator Badge
Creators can earn a **BlerdArt Verified** badge by:
1. Demonstrating consistent high-quality art output
2. Committing to community standards in writing
3. Admin review & approval

**What It Means:**
- ✓ Verified creator has been background-checked for authentic artistic practice
- ✓ This creator affirms all work is human-created (or AI disclosed)
- ✓ This creator has committed to community guidelines

**Why It Matters:**
- Helps viewers distinguish between proven creators and new accounts
- Protects against impersonation or stolen portfolios
- Builds trust in the creator economy

### Reporting & Moderation
**Report Mechanisms:**
- Flag button on every video, comment, and user page
- Direct moderation contact: `trust@nichestream.example.com`
- Confidential form on Trust & Safety page

**Moderation Approach:**
- **Restorative First:** For first-time minor violations, we offer dialogue over deletion. (Example: Creator unknowingly used asset without attribution. We facilitate apology + proper credit.)
- **Escalate as Needed:** Repeat offenders or severe violations → immediate action
- **Transparent After Action:** Creators receive explanation of any removals or restrictions within 24 hours

**Appeals:**
Creators can appeal any action via `appeals@nichestream.example.com` with additional context within 30 days.

## Harassment & Hate Speech Response Protocol

### If You Experience Harassment:
1. **Document:** Screenshot the messages (they may be deleted)
2. **Report:** Use flag button or email `trust@nichestream.example.com`
3. **Tell Mods:** Include as much context as possible (username, timestamps, is this an organized campaign?)
4. **Get Support:** We'll connect you with resources and take action

### If You Commit Harassment:
1. **First Offense:** Warning + content restriction (video stays public but limited distribution)
2. **Second Offense:** 7-day suspension
3. **Third Offense:** 30-day suspension + verification hold
4. **Fourth+:** Permanent ban

**Exceptions for Severity:**
- Slurs, death threats, doxxing, or sexual harassment = potential immediate ban
- Coordinated harassment campaigns = all involved accounts banned
- Targeting minors = immediate ban + potential law enforcement referral

## Copyright & Attribution

### Your Rights as a Creator
- ✓ You own all original work you create
- ✓ You control how your work is used (via license you choose)
- ✓ You can monetize your work on NicheStream
- ✓ You retain rights even if your account is deleted

### Fair Use & Transformative Work
We allow:
- ✓ Critique/commentary videos that quote original work
- ✓ Reaction videos with clips from other creators (linked with permission)
- ✓ "Draw my OC" challenges (remixing characters)

- ✓ Parody and satire

### Attribution
When using someone else's work:
```
"Based on [Creator Name]'s [Original Work Title]"
Video: [Link]
Permission: [Link to license or explicit grant]
```

## Accessibility & Inclusion

### Closed Captions & Descriptions
We encourage but don't require:
- ✓ Closed captions for spoken dialogue
- ✓ Audio descriptions for process videos
- ✓ Transcripts for tutorials

**Why:** Deaf/HoH creators deserve full access. Auto-captions via Workers AI available for free to all creators.

### Content Warnings
For community wellness, consider warning labels:
- CW: Sexual content
- CW: Graphic violence
- CW: Strobe/flashing lights
- CW: Self-harm discussion

Warnings don't hide content—they give viewers choice.

### Mental Health
**Creator Burnout:** Taking breaks is healthy. The platform will be here when you need it.

**Criticism Fatigue:** Comments can be disabled per-video. No one owes engagement.

**Crisis Support:** Links to [Crisis Text Line](https://www.crisistextline.org/), [SAMHSA Helpline](https://www.samhsa.gov/public-awareness/nami/treatment-referral-routing-service) available in Help section.

## Transparency & Trust

### Platform Policies
NicheStream commits to:
- Publish moderation stats quarterly (removals, bans, appeals granted)
- Explain policy changes 30 days before implementation
- Create a creator advisory board for policy input
- Be honest about limitations (e.g., "We can't moderate all content in real-time")

### Creator Data
- ✓ You own your data; export it anytime
- ✓ We don't sell your data to third parties
- ✓ Analytics show views, engagement, earnings (your control)
- ✓ Deletion: Request full account erasure; all videos & data removed within 90 days
